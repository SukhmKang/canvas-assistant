• How To Generate A Canvas API Key
  • 1
    Log into your Canvas account.
  • 2
    Click Account in the sidebar and click Settings.
  • 3
    Scroll down to the Approved Integrations section and click New Access Token.
  • 4
    Fill in a Purpose and an (optional) Expiration Date and click Generate Token.
  • 5
    Copy your token down and save it somewhere you can easily access it.
  • 6
    Load up Canvas Helper and you're ready to go!
